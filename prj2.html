3D SPACE LANDER

A 3d simulation game. The goal of this application is to simulate the motion of the lunar lander.

Concepts Supported:

checkBasic Physics Engine: Uses kinematic physics.

checkOctree for collision detection and mouse-ray detection.

checkParticle System for simulating the thruster flame.

Controls:

To Switch Cameras:

F1 - Main Cam
F2 - Front Cam
F3 - Bottom Cam
F4 - Track Cam
Two different ways to control the spacecraft:
Arrow Keys:

Up for +y
Down for -y
Left for -x
Right for +x
Ctrl + Up for +z
Ctrl + Down for -z
Mouse Click:

Press h - to hover
Then click anywhere on the ground
Other Controls
Press s - screenshot
